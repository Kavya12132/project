Hunt Middle School

Hunt Middle School (HMS) is a top rated public middle school located in frisco, texas. Its principal is Mrs.Williams and there are many electives but only 5 for 6th graders including the Fine arts section: band, orchestra, choir and theater, along with all of this there is art as another elective. In 7th grade you get everything already listed for 6th graders as well as art 2, masters band, advanced orchestra, advanced choir, theater 2, spanish, engineering, robotics, avid and skills for living. for 8th grade you have everything listed in 6th and 7th grade other than spanish 2, art 3 and theater 3, 6th graders pick one elective and 7th graders pick 2 while 8th graders pick 3, 7th graders also arent able to pick any athletics while in 7th and 8th grade you can pick athletics, tennis and cheer.
